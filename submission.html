Submission Information

Submission Details

The workshop invites original, unpublished work that explores biomechanics-guided multimodal understanding of movement. All submissions should be formatted according to BMVC style guidelines and include a clear description of the scientific contribution.

Submission process

Paper submission: Submit your manuscript through the workshop track on the OpenReview or conference submission portal.
Review: Each paper will receive peer review based on originality, technical quality, relevance, and clarity.
Notification: Authors will receive review decisions and revision instructions after the review period.

Formatting requirements

  • Use the BMVC paper format and style guidelines.
  • Prepare a PDF manuscript with sufficient detail for review.
  • Include figures, tables, and references in the main paper.
  • Supplementary material is welcome where useful.
